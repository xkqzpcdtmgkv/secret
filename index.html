A new, free and lightweight DAW is here. Welcome, Lithium, an all new DAW by world renowned producer (that's a lie lol) Intergalaxity who doesn't know anything about coding bru anyways I love the web design of AO3, rest assured I have never signed up for it or read anything on there, oh dear god. I just love the simplicity of the design of the front page is all, so here it is, i guess.

Free, forever.

Lithium is a DAW free of charge, available to download whenever, wherever you would like to start making the music you like.

Lightweight like a feather.

Lithium aims to be a lightweight DAW that is soft on your hardware so you can produce while doing other tasks you enjoy.

Easy to use.

Lithium also aims to be very user-friendly unlike other major DAWs, we understand that ideas can be captured in seconds, and with our DAW, you can make that idea a reality with how fast you can navigate through our DAW.

So what are you waiting for?

Get started today by signing up or logging on the top right corner of the header, then download our DAW in the downloads page.
